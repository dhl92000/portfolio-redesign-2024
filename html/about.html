My Story.

Hi, I'm Dahee!

I am a fullstack engineer that is fascinated by both frontend and backend development.

I joined a major airline in 2016 and became addicted to travelling and exploring new places. After developing a knack for connecting with customers and an interest in the Toronto real estate market, I started my own business as a real estate salesperson. I was able to build a clientele of repeat clients and referrals, creating a marketing plan and online presence, and everything in between.

While I was working in both industries, I found myself wondering how to improve the technologies my clients were using. There was a trend for minimal user interfaces, but some clients who were more senior had a lot of trouble using these. Similarly, at the airport, I would witness passengers staring blankly at the self-check-in terminals, not knowing what to do.

This intrigue into how these applications were made was where my transition to a career in software development started and I have been programming ever since.
